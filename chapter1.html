Chapter 1: Summer Fun & Hard Work ☀️

In a sunny meadow, Andy the Ant worked hard every day, carrying grains and berries to his cozy home. He hummed a little tune as he prepared for the winter ahead.

Nearby, Gary the Grasshopper loved to sing, dance, and enjoy the warm sunshine. "Andy, why do you work so hard?" Gary laughed.

"Come play with me! The sun is shining!" Andy shook his head. "I must prepare for winter, Gary. When it's cold, we need food to eat."

But Gary just shrugged and continued hopping among the flowers. He spent the summer singing, laughing, and playing.
